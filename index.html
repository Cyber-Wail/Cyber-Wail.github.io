New York City, USA

5 Things to Do

  • Visit Times Square
  • Explore Central Park
  • See a Broadway show
  • Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • Take a ferry to the Statue of Liberty

5 Foods to Eat

  • New York-style pizza
  • Bagels with lox and cream cheese
  • Cheesecake
  • Hot dogs
  • Pastrami on rye

Santorini, Greece

5 Things to Do

  • Watch the sunset in Oia
  • Visit the Red Beach
  • Explore the ancient ruins of Akrotiri
  • Take a wine tour
  • Relax in hot springs

5 Foods to Eat

  • Souvlaki
  • Moussaka
  • Greek Salad
  • Fava Me Koukia
  • Tomatokeftedes

Kyoto, Japan

5 Things to Do

  • Visit Kinkaku-ji (The Golden Pavilion)
  • Explore Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Walk through Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Experience a tea ceremony
  • Visit Nijo Castle

5 Foods to Eat

  • Kaiseki Ryori
  • Yudofu (Hot Tofu)
  • Shojin Ryori (Buddhist Cuisine)
  • Matcha (Green Tea) Sweets
  • Kyo-Wagashi (Kyoto Sweets)

Paris, France

5 Things to Do

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Walk along the Champs-Élysées
  • Visit Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Relax in Luxembourg Gardens

5 Foods to Eat

  • Croissants
  • Escargots
  • French Onion Soup
  • Macarons
  • Coq au Vin

Maldives

5 Things to Do

  • Snorkeling and Diving
  • Relax on the beach
  • Visit Male, the capital
  • Go island hopping
  • Experience water sports

5 Foods to Eat

  • Garudhiya (Fish Soup)
  • Mas Huni (Shredded Smoked Tuna)
  • Fihunu Mas (Grilled Fish)
  • Hedhikaa (Snacks)
  • Saagu Bondibai (Sago Pudding)
